What is Outlier AI?

+

Outlier AI is a platform that connects AI companies with skilled experts to help train and improve AI systems. We provide opportunities for individuals to contribute their expertise in various domains such as data annotation, content moderation, and specialized knowledge work while earning competitive pay on a flexible schedule.

How do I get started?

+

Getting started is easy:

  1. Create an account by clicking "Sign Up" on our homepage
  2. Complete your profile and skills assessment
  3. Browse available projects that match your skills
  4. Complete the training for your chosen project
  5. Start working and earning!

What qualifications do I need?

+

Requirements vary by project, but generally we look for:

  • Fluency in English (additional languages are a plus for certain projects)
  • Reliable internet connection
  • Computer or laptop (some tasks can be completed on mobile devices)
  • Attention to detail and commitment to quality
  • Specialized knowledge for certain projects (e.g., medical, legal, technical)

How much can I earn?

+

Earnings vary based on the complexity of tasks, your skill level, and time commitment. Most contributors earn between $15-30 per hour, with specialized experts earning more. You'll always see the pay rate before accepting a project, and payments are processed bi-weekly.

How many hours can I work?

+

One of the benefits of Outlier AI is flexibility. You can work as little as a few hours per week or up to full-time hours, depending on your availability and the number of tasks available in your skill areas. Some projects have minimum weekly hour requirements, which will be clearly stated before you join.

How do I get paid?

+

We offer multiple payment options including PayPal, direct bank transfer, and Wise. Payments are processed every two weeks for all work completed and approved during the previous period. You can track your earnings in real-time on your dashboard.

Still have questions?

Our support team is here to help. Reach out to us and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
